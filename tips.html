Cyber Security Tips

  • 1. Use Strong Passwords: Create complex passwords with a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols. Example: P@ssw0rd!2023
  • 2. Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA): Add an extra layer of security by using 2FA. Example: Use Google Authenticator.
  • 3. Be Wary of Phishing Scams: Do not click on suspicious links or attachments in emails. Example: Verify the sender's email address before clicking.
  • 4. Keep Software Updated: Regularly update your operating system and applications. Example: Turn on automatic updates.
  • 5. Use Antivirus Software: Install and maintain reputable antivirus software. Example: Norton, McAfee, or Kaspersky.
  • 6. Backup Your Data: Regularly backup important data to avoid data loss. Example: Use external hard drives or cloud storage.
  • 7. Secure Your Wi-Fi Network: Use a strong password and encryption for your Wi-Fi. Example: WPA3 encryption.
  • 8. Be Careful with Public Wi-Fi: Avoid accessing sensitive information on public Wi-Fi. Example: Use a VPN when necessary.
  • 9. Educate Yourself: Stay informed about the latest cyber threats and how to avoid them. Example: Follow cybersecurity blogs and news.
  • 10. Monitor Your Accounts: Regularly check your financial and online accounts for suspicious activity. Example: Set up account alerts.
